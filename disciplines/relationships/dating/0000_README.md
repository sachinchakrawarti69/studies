# Dating

> Learn how to build healthy, respectful, and meaningful romantic relationships.

---

## 📚 Course Overview

This section covers the fundamentals of dating, emotional maturity, communication, trust, boundaries, and developing healthy relationships.

The goal is to understand dating from psychological, emotional, social, and practical perspectives rather than myths or stereotypes.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this section, you will be able to:

- Understand what dating is and its purpose.
- Build healthy communication skills.
- Recognize green flags and red flags.
- Set healthy boundaries.
- Develop trust and emotional maturity.
- Handle conflicts respectfully.
- Cope with breakups in a healthy way.
- Make informed relationship decisions.
